    About us

    Bites of Paradise is focused on traditional and continental recipes

    Bites of Paradise is owned two Kano sisters, Hajiya and Hajjo, who are passionate about owning a restaurant that offers a tantalizing receipe and service that will be delightful and memorable to their customers. Hajjo does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic local to incorporate additional cuisines from the other continents.

    To craft the menu, our chef, Hajiya relies on family recipes and her experience as a chef in Italy.

    • The Bites of Paradise has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices.
    • Italian, English, Arabian and Hausa menus that suits all times of the day and season.

    A Traditional British Delight

    Transport yourself to the streets of Nasarrawa, Kano with our traditional English breakfast. Indulge in a hearty meal that combines the best of British flavors and culinary traditions. Savor the aroma of sizzling bacon, relish the rich taste of grilled sausages, and delight in the golden goodness of perfectly fried eggs.

    Rise and Shine to a Delicious Start

    Our breakfast menu at Bites of Paradise offers a delightful array of options to kickstart your day on a scrumptious note. From fluffy pancakes drizzled with maple syrup to golden French toast dusted with powdered sugar, our breakfast dishes are crafted with love and care.

    Midday Delights for Every Palate

    At Bites of Paradise, our lunch menu offers a diverse selection of dishes designed to satisfy all taste preferences. From light and refreshing salads bursting with seasonal ingredients to hearty sandwiches piled high with premium meats and cheeses, our lunch options cater to every craving.

    Classic Italian Pasta Perfection

    Dive into the world of authentic Italian cuisine with our exquisite spaghetti dishes. Made with al dente pasta cooked to perfection and paired with a variety of flavorful sauces, our spaghetti creations are a testament to culinary artistry.

    Soul-Warming Bowls of Comfort

    Warm your heart and soul with our comforting selection of soups at Bites of Paradise. Bursting with flavors and crafted with care, our soups are the perfect remedy for a chilly day or a quick pick-me-up.
